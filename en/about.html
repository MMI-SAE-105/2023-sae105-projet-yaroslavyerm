About

I am Yaroslav Yermakov, first year student at MMi of Montbeliard.

This website is created as my first solo project. The topic of the website revolves around the quote “All the bright precious things fade so fast, and they don’t come back", from movie “The Great Gatsby” (2013). I chose them because I like the movie for its visual aesthetics and message it conveys. The song I picked is “Young and Beautifull” by Lana Del Rey, because it is movie’s soundtrack that perfectly fits its energy and because I highly appreciate Del Rey’s works.
